UYAR Engineering & Construction is a company founded by two experienced engineers Pon Ilango and T.M. Nagarajan, who have each amassed 25 years of individual international industry experience across the UAE, Malaysia, and India.

UYAR is committed to providing construction services with at most adherence to Quality, Health & Safety, and Environment. We do this through the combination of an open relationship with our employees based on mutual trust, transparency, accountability, and discipline.

Our company is empowered by the presence of qualified and experienced engineers, professionals, associates, technicians, and skilled workers who are well-versed in the execution of all kinds of civil engineering projects, with a stubborn commitment to quality and project schedule to the entire satisfaction of all our esteemed clients.

We undertake diversified construction projects including individual Villas, multi-storied Residential and Commercial Buildings, IT Parks, Institutional Buildings, Healthcare buildings, Industrial Buildings, Warehouses, Precast, and Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB).

We are also offering project-based services including Project Management Consultancy (PMC), Contracts Administration, and Estimation & Costing.

We don't just create masterpieces in the construction and leave it to the Clients. We realize that the continuous upkeep of the asset is as important as creating it.

Hence we extended our Value Added Services to ensure the property gives continuous and maximum value undertaken upon post-completion.

We liaise between Clients and various allied agencies to completely customize the property for the most utilization and 100% satisfaction of our Clients.

Operating throughout Coimbatore, Salem, and Tamil Nadu, Uyar is dedicated to delivering projects that set new standards in the field of construction.
